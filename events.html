Paperenza( Paper Presentation )

Rules

  • Any technical stream is encouraged.
  • Candidates send their paper in soft copy to scape2k23@gmail.com before 9th April 2023 .
  • Spot entries are not allowed.
  • Accepted papers will be acknowledged to respective mail ID.
  • The presentation time is 6 minutes and two minutes for questions & answers.
  • The oral presentation should be in the form of Microsoft Power Point slides.
  • Selected candidates should bring two hard copies of the paper during the presentation time.

    • Note: Name should be mentioned in the filename.
